Our Story

It all started with a simple question: "Ma ano ulam?" The answer was always the same: Manok na Chicken. We decided to stop overcomplicating dinner and start perfecting the grill. Founded in 2024, our shop began as a small roadside stand dedicated to one thing—making the juiciest, smokiest chicken that stays true to its name.
